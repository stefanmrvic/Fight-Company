Fight Games XI

Home / Events / Fight Games XI

The most massive amateur MMA competition and the Championship of Vojvodina, better known as "Fight Games", has been postponed due to the tightening of measures due to the current situation with the virus. Fight Games 11 was supposed to be held on November 29 in Vrbas, but due to the epidemiological situation, it will be held in February, when the conditions for such a thing are met.

This year, Fight Games is celebrating its 10th anniversary and still holds the record when it comes to amateur competition on the territory of the Republic of Serbia with the most registered competitors. The organizer of this well-known amateur competition is Petar Tunguz and Unity Fight Team from Vrbas.
