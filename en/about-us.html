Our Philosophy

Everyone learns at a different pace, if something doesn't go your way a thousand times, it will on the thousand and the first atempt. We need to focus on the things we can influence and with time and effort to correct them and bring them to the desired level.

About The Club

Fight Company is a newly opened club made by Savo Lazić i Vaso Bakočević, with the goal of setting a new standard in the field and taking the MMA knowledge to the next level. Although Fight Company is a relatively new MMA club, the Fight Company team consists of longtime experienced fighters and coaches. It doesn't matter if it's regional fights, short notice fights in russia, or even the bareknuckle in the most prestigious european organization KSW, our fighters will be there.

Our team counts over 118 professional fights, and over 77 wins. Some of the organizations that our fighters participated in include: Megdan, FFC, SBC, FNC, FNG, KSW, Bellator, and many more.
